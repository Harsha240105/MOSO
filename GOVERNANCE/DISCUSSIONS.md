# MOSO AI — Discussion Categories

GitHub Discussions is the community hub for MOSO AI. All discussions are public
and open to everyone. Please follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

---

## Categories

### 📢 Announcements
- **Description:** Official news, releases, and updates from the MOSO AI team
- **Format:** Announcement (only maintainers can post)
- **Used for:** Release announcements, roadmap updates, major changes

### 💬 General
- **Description:** General discussion about MOSO AI
- **Format:** Open discussion
- **Used for:** Anything that doesn't fit elsewhere

### 💡 Ideas & Feedback
- **Description:** Share your ideas and feedback for MOSO AI
- **Format:** Open discussion
- **Used for:** Feature suggestions, improvement ideas, user experience feedback

### 🐛 Bug Reports (Redirect)
- **Description:** Bug reports should be filed as GitHub Issues
- **Format:** Question / Answer
- **Used for:** Directing to issue tracker for confirmed bugs

### ❓ Q&A
- **Description:** Questions about using, building, or contributing to MOSO AI
- **Format:** Question / Answer
- **Used for:** Troubleshooting, how-to questions, clarification

### 🔬 Research
- **Description:** Discussions about AI research, model selection, and technical architecture
- **Format:** Open discussion
- **Used for:** Academic discussion, paper references, technical deep dives

### 🎨 Show & Tell
- **Description:** Show what you've built with or for MOSO AI
- **Format:** Open discussion
- **Used for:** Community projects, demos, use cases

### 🤝 Contributing
- **Description:** Discussions about the contribution process
- **Format:** Open discussion
- **Used for:** Process questions, mentor requests, contributor onboarding

### 🛡️ Privacy & Security
- **Description:** Discuss privacy and security aspects of MOSO AI
- **Format:** Open discussion
- **Used for:** Privacy questions, security considerations, threat modeling

---

## Guidelines

1. **Search before posting** — Check if your topic already exists
2. **Be respectful** — Follow the Code of Conduct
3. **Stay on topic** — Use the appropriate category
4. **No support for unofficial builds** — We only support the official release
5. **No spam or self-promotion** — Except in Show & Tell

---

## Moderation

Discussions are moderated by MOSO AI maintainers. Violations of the Code of Conduct
may result in posts being hidden or users being blocked.
